How to find your Steam ID


1. Using a browser

  1. Open Steam community in a browser, and sign in.
  2. After signing in, click on your picture in the top right hand corner to open your steam profile.
  3. Copy the numbers after /profiles/, this is your Steam ID.
    {% load static %}

    Don't worry if yours looks different, this means you created a vanity url in your steam profile settings at some point. This will still work.

  4. Go back to the home page and paste your steam ID.

2. Using Steam mobile app

  1. Open Steam on your phone (and of course, be logged in).
  2. Tap your profile photo in the top right corner.
  3. Scroll down and look for the Account Details page.
  4. After opening your account details, look directly under where it says {your steam account name}'s Account. You will see 'Steam ID:'.
  5. Go back to the home page on this website and type in your steam ID.
